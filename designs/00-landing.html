How Janus works

One door. Both sides covered.

01

Branded intake

Your logo, your link. Mobile-first form, e-signature, applicant pays the screening fee up front via Stripe.

02

Identity first

Selfie + ID match before a single paid report runs. Stops synthetic-identity fraud at the door.

03

Credit · criminal · eviction

Pulled from a national screening bureau. Returned and parsed automatically — no PDF wrangling.

04

Income, the modern way

Bank + payroll verification via Plaid. Paystub upload as fallback. Multi-source income calculated automatically.

05

References without phone tag

Auto-emailed structured forms to current landlord and employer. Responses live in the dossier.

06

Decision with reasoning

Policy-driven recommendation with cited evidence. Override anytime. Every override logged for fair-housing review.

Policies, not gut calls

Configure thresholds once. Apply them across every property.

Set your income-to-rent ratio, minimum credit floor, eviction lookback, criminal lookback by category, and occupancy cap. Janus applies them consistently — and shows every applicant exactly which threshold drove the recommendation.

Fair-housing friendly: every decision is auditable, every override is timestamped and reasoned.
